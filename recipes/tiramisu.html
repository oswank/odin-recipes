Classic Tiramisu

Description + Ingredients

This is an easy recipe for Tiramisu: a rich and creamy dessert that is made with ladyfingers (I use Lotus Biscoff cookies) soaked in espresso or strong coffee and rum, eggs, heavy cream, sugar, mascarpone, and unsweetened cocoa powder.

Ingredients

For the cream:

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup (100 grams) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup (227 grams) mascarpone, I use the entire tub of BelGioioso Mascarpone Cheese as it is perfect measurements
